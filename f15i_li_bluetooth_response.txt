Regarding your question about wireless Bluetooth headphones compatibility:

The F15i Li supports wired headphones only, via its built-in 3.5mm headphone output. When headphones are connected, the built-in speakers automatically mute, allowing for silent practice.

While the F15i Li does feature Bluetooth functionality, this is designed for:
- Connecting to your phone/device to play backing tracks through the amp's speakers
- Controlling the amp via the MOOER iAMP app
- Wireless footswitch pairing

Key features related to audio output:
- 3.5mm headphone output for silent practice
- 2x2-inch stereo speakers (15W total power)
- Bluetooth connectivity for audio playback from external devices
- Line output interfaces for connecting to external amplification equipment

If you're looking for wireless headphone functionality, you would need to use a separate Bluetooth audio transmitter connected to the F15i Li's headphone output.
