F15i Li 固件更新失败（进度卡在37%）

Dear customer,

Thank you for reaching out to MOOER Support.

We are sorry to hear that your F15i Li encountered an issue during the firmware update (stuck at approximately 37%). This is a situation that can sometimes occur. Please follow the troubleshooting steps below:

1. Use a better USB cable
Use the original USB-C cable that came with the F15i Li, or a thicker, higher-quality USB-C cable capable of carrying higher current. Avoid using external USB hubs, adapters, or thin/low-quality third-party cables — these are a common cause of update failures.

2. Use a USB 3.0 port
Connect the unit directly to a USB 3.0 port on your computer (the blue port). Please avoid USB 2.0 ports, USB hubs, or docking stations during the update.

3. Fully charge the battery before updating
Before attempting the update, charge the F15i Li using a 5V/2A power adapter for at least 4 hours. A low battery during a firmware update can cause the device to shut down mid-update and fail. Make sure the battery is well charged before proceeding.

If the unit still does not respond after following these steps, please let us know.

Best regards,
MOOER Audio Support Team

macOS Error: "Failed to parse the MR file"
This error usually occurs when the software is run directly from the mounted Disk Image (.dmg) or the Downloads folder rather than being properly installed. To fix this:

Close the Mooer Studio software.

Drag the Mooer Studio icon into your Mac's Applications folder.

Eject the installer (.dmg) image from your desktop.

Launch the software directly from the Applications folder and try the update again.

If the problem persists after trying these steps, please let us know your computer's operating system version, and we will assist you further.

App / firmware version prompt guide - iAMP and MOOER Cloud

Dear customer,

Thank you for choosing our products. To help resolve the app or firmware version prompt, please follow the steps below according to your product and the message shown in the app.

For iAMP series products, such as F15i, Hornet 15i/30i, SD10i/30i, etc.:

1. If your app has already been updated to v1.6.0 but still shows "APP version too low", please uninstall the current app and then download and install it again from the official app store. After reinstalling, reconnect your device.

2. If your app has not been updated to the latest version, please update it to v1.6.0 first. After the update is complete, reopen the app and try again.

3. If the app shows "firmware version too low", this is a normal firmware update reminder. Please follow the in-app instructions to update your device firmware to the latest version.

For MOOER Cloud related products, including GE100 Pro and GS1000:

If your GE100 Pro firmware is already V1.3.2, or your GS1000 firmware is already V1.2.7, but the app still shows "firmware version too low", please uninstall the current app, reinstall it from the official app store, log in again, and reconnect your device.

If the issue still remains after these steps, please send us your device model, firmware version, app version, and a screenshot of the message shown in the app. We will help check it further.

Thanks again and have a nice day!
